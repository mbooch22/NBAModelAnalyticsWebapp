Objectives

The predictions of this Machine Learning Model (MLM) are used against the corresponding Sportsbook Odds. If the prediction of trained MLM falls within a certain threshold*, then that "bet" will count towards the MLMs overall record.

The objective of this application is to display how the predictive part of the MLM is performing against the odds set by Vegas Sportsbooks. The goal of this MLM is to have a record of 60% or greater.

* When the MLM gets trained, certain thresholds are given for each data point the MLM is trying to predict. These help exclude any outlier predictions and keep the MLM at the highest accuracy. These thresholds will remain proprietary.
