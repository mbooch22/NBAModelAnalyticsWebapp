Webscraping Details

Using a separate proprietary web scraping application, NBA stats over the past decade were retrieved from every game played.

*Raw Average stats were calculated by taking the raw average of the combined statistics for a given team/player. Home and Away statistics were also filtered to get a better understanding of Home Advantage. Adjusted stats were then calculated to compensate for data imbalances due to varying opponents' and other outlying figures.

    Types of Statistics inlcuded:
  • Points Per Game (per Team and Player) (Raw Average and Adjusted*)
  • Rebounds Per Game (per Team and Player) (Defensive and Offensive) (Raw Average and Adjusted*)
  • Assists Per Game (per Team and Player) (Raw Average and Adjusted*)
  • Turnovers Per Game (per Team and Player) (Raw Average and Adjusted*)
  • Tempo Per Game (per Team and Player) (Raw Average and Adjusted*)
  • Offensive Efficiency Per Game (per Team and Player) (Raw Average and Adjusted*)
  • Defensive Efficiency Per Game (per Team and Player) (Raw Average and Adjusted*)
  • Field Goal Attempts Per Game (per Team and Player) (Raw Average and Adjusted*)
  • Field Goals Made Per Game (per Team and Player) (Raw Average and Adjusted*)
  • Field Goal Percentage Per Game (per Team and Player) (Raw Average and Adjusted*)
  • Effective Field Goal Efficiency Per Game (per Team and Player) (Raw Average and Adjusted*)
  • 3 Point Field Goal Attempts Per Game (per Team and Player) (Raw Average and Adjusted*)
  • 3 Point Field Goals Made Per Game (per Team and Player) (Raw Average and Adjusted*)
  • 3 Point Field Goal Percentage Per Game (per Team and Player) (Raw Average and Adjusted*)
  • Free Throw Attempts Per Game (per Team and Player) (Raw Average and Adjusted*)
  • etc..

All of these statistics were located in a central data file to feed into the Machine Learning Model in order to get predictive analysis.
